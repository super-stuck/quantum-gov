4. **Game-Theoretic Analysis**: How do quantum strategies affect Nash equilibria in voting games?

#### Theoretical Framework
```
Quantum Governance Complexity Class (QGC):
- Definition: Problems solvable by quantum algorithms for governance in polynomial time
- Relationship to classical complexity: QGC ⊆ BQP, potentially QGC ⊊ PPAD
```

- **Quantum Operators**: Governance actions as unitary transformations U
- **Measurement Theory**: Policy outcomes as quantum measurements
- **Entanglement Quantification**: Democratic consensus through quantum correlations

#### Expected Deliverables

2. **Complexity Analysis**: Computational complexity characterization of quantum governance
3. **Mathematical Framework**: Axiomatic foundation for quantum democratic theory
4. **Verification Protocols**: Methods for verifying quantum governance computations
