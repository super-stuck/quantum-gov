## üöÄ Getting Started

### Prerequisites
1. **GitHub Account**: Create an account at https://github.com
2. **Basic Git Knowledge**: Familiarize yourself with Git version control
3. **Research Background**: Understanding of democratic theory, quantum computing, or related fields is helpful but not required

### Initial Steps
1. **Explore the Repository**: Visit https://github.com/super-stuck/quantum-gov
2. **Read Documentation**: Review existing research materials and technical specifications
3. **Check Issues**: Look for open issues that match your interests and expertise
4. **Join Discussions**: Participate in project discussions and planning

## üìã Contribution Process

### 1. Issue Reporting
- **Bug Reports**: Report errors or inconsistencies in documentation
- **Feature Requests**: Suggest new research directions or improvements
- **Questions**: Ask questions about the project or research

### 2. Pull Requests
1. **Fork** the repository
2. **Create a branch** for your contribution
3. **Make your changes** with clear commit messages
4. **Submit a pull request** with detailed description
5. **Respond to feedback** during the review process

### 3. Research Contributions
1. **Propose** your research topic or improvement
2. **Discuss** approach with the team
3. **Collaborate** on research and documentation
4. **Review** and refine contributions
